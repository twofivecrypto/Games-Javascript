Future Features Overview

Data Injection - Insight at Your Fingertips

We understand the importance of empowering credit companies to make data-driven decisions with confidence. With Data Injection, developers from credit companies gain access to a suite of tools that provide invaluable insights into their past, present, and future performance.

Past Performance Analysis: HindSight

Data Scanning and Analysis: Credit companies can upload their historical financial reports and data into Astora's platform.

Performance Report: Our model scans this data and generates a comprehensive report highlighting past performance trends.

Comparison for Better Decision-Making

Comparison with Astora: HindSight then takes this analysis a step further by showing how Astora's involvement might have impacted past performance over the last 3-6 months.
